Who am I?

My name is Natalie Bamsch and I am a senior at California State East Bay. I am majoring in Graphic Design and enjoy sculpting, paint and drawing. Some of my favorite things to sculpt or draw are animals. I take inspiration from my pets as well as animals I find in nature. I also love to design minimalist artworks as well as more detailed and realistic works.
